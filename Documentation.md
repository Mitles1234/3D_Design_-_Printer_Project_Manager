## 1. Identifying & Defining
### 1.1 Problem Statement
With CAD and 3D printing becoming a growing industry, more people are using software, and design tools, catering around their unique needs. However, their is a distinct lack of tools that allow for the combination of tracking, and maintaining changes between these software mediums, aswell as managing the consumables and hardware useage during the design process.

This can lead to projects and designs taking longer to create, as significat features can be lost in older designs, and tips for printing these designs can be forgetten.

This means their is a significant market that is in need of a simple, effective manager of files, projects and tools, during the development cycle.

### 1.2 Project Purpose and Boundaries
This project is designed to manage:
 - Viewing / Interacting With 3D printer API's (Such as Mainsail, or Fluidd)
 - Management of 3D printers, including Maintainence Logs, Activity Usage, and Upgrades
 - Managing of Filaments in terms of Last Measured Humidity, and Usage through Designs
 - Storage of Iterations of Designs, Slicer Files, Stored in an Appropriately Stuctured File Tree
 - Built in Functionality with Orcaslicer, for Project Creation, and Printer Usage

An example of these elements would be recent projects, creation of new projects.
